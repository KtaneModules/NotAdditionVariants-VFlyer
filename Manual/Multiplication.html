On the Subject of Multiplication

Honestly, this is literally just multiplying 3 digit numbers. I have no idea why anybody thought that this would be fun, challenging, or interesting to put on a bomb.

Compute the product on the display and enter it using the numbered buttons to solve the module. Use the gray ‘M’ button to cycle through 10 numbers, the red ‘C’ button to clear your input, and the ‘×’ button to submit it.

Notes:

  1. The input display can only have a maximum of 30 digits.
  2. The first number in the cycle is ALWAYS colored gray.
  3. Incorrect submissions will result in a strike and resets the module with most likely 10 new numbers.
